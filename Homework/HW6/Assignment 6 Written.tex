            Divide both sides by c, which yields $\frac{cap(\gamma)}{c} \leq \frac{cap(\xi)}{c}$\\
            This means that $\gamma$ is a min-cut in the original graph, which is a contradiction\\
            Therefore, the min-cuts stay the same\\
